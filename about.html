ABOUT US: Slice of Pizza House

🍕Slice of Heaven Pizzeria was founded in Canada in a little town called Barrie in 2024. Our founder, Lionel Messi, brought his secret family recipes straight from Rosario, Argentina. With passion and a wood-fired oven, he created a little slice of heaven right here in our cozy corner of the city.

🌟What We Do: At Pizza House, we’re more than just a pizzeria. We’re a culinary experience. Our dough is hand-kneaded daily, and our sauces simmer with love. Each pizza is a masterpiece, topped with the freshest ingredients—locally sourced whenever possible. From classic Margheritas to adventurous BBQ chicken pies, we’ve got something for every palate.

🏆Awards and Accolades: Voted “Best Pizza Crust” by the City Foodies Association Featured in “Pizza Lovers’ Guide to New York” magazine Lionel Messi’s Marinara Sauce won the Golden Ladle Award at the Annual Sauce-Off Festival

🍽️Beyond Pizza: While our pizzas steal the spotlight, we also serve mouthwatering pasta dishes, crispy garlic knots, and indulgent tiramisu. Pair your meal with a glass of Chianti or a cold Peroni beer—it’s like a mini vacation to Argentina.

📍Visit Us: Come share a slice with us! You’ll find Slice of Heaven Pizzeria at Georgian Drive, where the aroma of bubbling cheese and fresh basil beckons you inside.

Whether you’re a pizza aficionado or a first-time visitor, we can’t wait to serve you. Buon appetito!🍽️ If you have any question, contact us below! 😊
